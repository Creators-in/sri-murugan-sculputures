About Us

The Best

Sri Murugan sculpture

Sri Murugan sculpture is a leading sculpting and wood carving firm in mamallapuram which has a good reputation among customers and functioning successfully in its crystal year with fifteen years of expertise. Our ultimate aim is to satisfy customers by providing customized and perfectly finished sculptures. Our sculpting techniques include molded, cast, carved and assembled. Various mediums used are wood, cement, stone, and metal. Generally we use carving and modelling for durable sculptures. We make sculptures of various gods and goddesses whole heartedly as per the requirement and basic idols for customers. We have our clients from overall india and some overseas clients from malaysia and singapore.

Our Business make sure Cost affordability, Quality of products, On time delivery, Customized sculptures, uniquely designed wood carvings. We also do wood carving and painting. Basswood and oak are our primary wood for carving and we make sure the durability and tensile strength of doors. Customized designs and variety of door carvings is our speciality. Our woodworking and sculpting team make sure the individuality of customized designs to each and every customer.
