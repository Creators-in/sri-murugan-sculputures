Sri Murugan sculpture

  • Sri Murugan sculpture is a leading sculpting and wood carving firm in mamallapuram which has a good reputation among customers and functioning successfully in its crystal year with fifteen years of expertise. Our ultimate aim is to satisfy customers by providing customized and perfectly finished sculptures. Our sculpting techniques include molded, cast, carved and assembled. Various mediums used are wood, cement, stone, and metal. Generally we use carving and modelling for durable sculptures.

  • We make sculptures of various gods and goddesses whole heartedly as per the requirement and basic idols for customers. We have our clients from overall india and some overseas clients from malaysia and singapore.
